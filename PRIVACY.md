# Elite Eden Bot Privacy Policy.

Joining the Elite Eden Discord Server ("Elite Eden", "we", "us") constitutes your acceptance of this policy. If you have any requests, legal or otherwise, please send a DM to @cootshk over Discord.

## Data We Collect

We process all messages that get sent in the Elite Eden. Discord provides us with the following data:
- Your Unique Discord User Identifier (User ID)
- Your Discord profile, including but not limited to: your username, nickname, profile, and banner.
- Any messages you send within the Elite Eden or affiliated Discords.

The following data is stored by us:
- Your Discord profile picture and nickname
- Your User ID

Discord stores additional data about you, which you can find on their privacy policy [here](https://discord.com/privacy/).

## What We Use Your Data For.

#### **We do not sell or share your data with anyone.**

Your data is used soley for the purpose of app functionality.

## Legal Opt-Outs

If you would like to excersize your legal rights in your place of residence, please leave the Elite Eden Discord server, and then send a DM over Discord to @cootshk with your request.
